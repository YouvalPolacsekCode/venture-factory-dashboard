How to read this page

The factory finds problems, scores them, and (later) builds the best ones. Here's what the labels mean and when something needs you.

Stage — what the factory thinks of an idea

  • drop not worth pursuing — too weak to chase.
  • validate promising — gather more proof before building.
  • build strong enough to build into a real micro-service.
  • scale already working — pour in more.

Realistic for you (solo)

  • ✅ Yes a non-technical solo operator could build, sell & maintain it with this factory.
  • ⚠️ Not yet one leg is weak (it names which: build it / find who pays / get them to pay / run it hands-off).

When does something need you?

Only the “Action needed” box at the top. The factory drafts everything itself, but it must ask your approval before any real-world action — sending outreach emails, publishing a page, charging a card, or spending over the daily cap. If that box says “Nothing waiting on you,” there's nothing to do.

Right now the loop discovers & scores ideas but doesn't yet auto-build them (the build/launch steps come in the next phases), so expect “Action needed” to stay empty until then.
